INT. KITCHEN - DAY The kitchen is small but clean. Morning sunlight fills the room. ANNA (looking out the window) It's so quiet today. I feel like something is about to happen. MARK (enters quietly) Good morning. Did you sleep well? ANNA (startled) Oh—Mark. You scared me. (A moment of silence as they both look at the window.) MARK I didn't mean to. I just wanted to check on you. EXT. GARDEN - AFTERNOON LUCAS I told you this place would calm you down. (steps closer) LUCAS You don't have to decide today. Take your time. ANNA I know. But waiting doesn't make it easier. (A bird chirps loudly nearby.) INT. KITCHEN - EVENING The kitchen is darker now; only a lamp is on. ANNA I think… I finally know what to do.
